## Rationale

Activity-log, quantity, configuration, staging, clone, and deletion operations are append-only domain events; all changing state is read through as-of projections. QuantityRevisionInput includes revisioned measure, fractional value, units, stored label, and renderable text; QuantityRevisionReference contains a QuantityHandle plus QuantityRevisionHandle; ActivityLogInput contains activity-log fields; QuantityFieldMapping describes an item path, outer-row fallback path, and destination nested path. The pending-selection projection captures the later view-access filter, with visibility enforced by disclosure/projection rather than a central authority. Deletion operations are composite replayable events so the source's inverse cleanup and deletion cascades are auditable. Quantity revisions and quantity-type revisions preserve concurrent histories while their effective materialized values converge.
