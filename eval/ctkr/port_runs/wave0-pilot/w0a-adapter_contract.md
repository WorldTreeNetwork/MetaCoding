## Rationale

Inventory adjustments are append-only log events and inventory is an as-of projection: it filters non-done and future-dated records, partitions exact measure/unit pairs, and implements timestamp-inclusive reset replay with ID ordering. Quantity and quantity-type edits are immutable revision events with deterministic eventual LWW/HLC materialization while retaining competing history. Quantity creation, label behavior, migration precedence/skip behavior, and rendered plain-text normalization are exposed because the mined fixtures observe them directly.
